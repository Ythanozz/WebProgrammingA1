Simple Recipe for Grilled Cheese

In this recipe I will show you a simple way to make a nice grilled cheese sandwhich using few ingredients, grilled cheese is a simple dish that anyone can make even with the most simple ingredients, it can be eaten at any time of the day for you to enjoy.

Here are the ingredients needed for making a good sandwhich:

  • Bread
  • Cheese(Preferably more than 1 kind)
  • Butter
  • Optional: Tomato

I say get more than 1 kind because the more different kinds of cheese you get, the more tastier it becomes!

  • The Steps:
    1. Preheat Skillet
    2. Butter the bread
    3. Add your cheese
    4. Grill your sandwhich
    5. Slice clean and enjoy.
    6. (additionaly, you can top it off with cheese, then pan fry it until it turns crispy.)
